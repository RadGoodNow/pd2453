Ta gina, mallaka, ko samar (${build.brand}) kuma ba a fitar da su ta daban ba a karkashin sharuddan lasisin a bude software. Bayanin da ke ƙunshe a cikin Ayyukan kadara ce ta musamman ga ${build.brand} a duk duniya kuma har abada ko kamar yadda ya dace. Kafin isar da wannan na'ura ta tashar zuwa ga masu amfani na karshe, babu wani daban da zai canza, fasa, tarwatsa, ko goge duk wani fayil, tsari, software, ko shirin da ke cikin wannan na'urar, ko shigar da kowane manhajoji akan wannan tasha ba tare da izinin farko ba daga ${build.brand}. ${build.brand} yana ba mai amfani da doka ta wannan na'urar tasha yancin yin amfani da Ayyukan kawai a cikin iyakokin halalcin aikin na'urar. Mai amfani na karshe ba zai, shi kadai ko ta wani na daban ba, ya gyara, fasa, tarwatsa, ko goge duk wata software ta aikin asali, fayilolin tsarin, tsarin aiki, ko daukar nauyin fayilolin da ke cikin wannan na'urar tasha. Mai amfani na karshe zai dauki alhakin duka sakamakon da aka ambata a sama. ${build.brand} yana rike da hakkin bincika hakkokin doka idan irin wannan hali ya haifar da lalacewa ga ${build.brand}. Babu wani karin hakki da aka bayar a karkashin wannan, gami da amma ba'a iyakance shi ba, rarrabawa, haifuwa, gyara, da/ko watsawa.

    Hakkin mallakar fasaha na wasu daban
  1. 1. Lasisin bude-tushe

    Software din ta kunshi fayilolin software wadanda ke karkashin batu na wasu yarjejeniyoyin lasisin bude tushe. Irin wadannan fayilolin software na bude tushe suna karkashin batu na bayanin da aka ambata a wannan sashe da kuma wasu sharudda da dokoki, kuma an samar da su "kamar yadda yake" a cikin mafi girma gwargwadon yadda dokokin da suka dace suka ba da izini.
    Don fayilolin bude tushe da aka hada a cikin wannan Yarjejeniyar, da fatan za a duba hakkin mallaka da sharuddan lasisi masu dacewa a cikin sashin "Lasisi Bude Tushe" a cikin saitunan wannan Na'ura. Idan kana da wasu batutuwan gano madaidaicin hakkin mallaka da sharuddan lasisi, da fatan za a tuntubi sabis na abokan ciniki. Kana iya samun lasisin Apache 2.0 anan: http://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0.

  2. 2. Alamar kasuwanci ta wasu daban

    Alamar kasuwanci mai zuwa mallakar Access Advance ce kuma ba za a yi amfani da ita ba tare da izini ba.

  3. 3. Hakkokin mallakar fasaha da wasu daban ke kiyaye su

    Kana iya amfani da wannan na'urar don samun damar kayayyaki da ayyuka na wasu daban, wadanda ke da hakkin hana shiga mara izini.
